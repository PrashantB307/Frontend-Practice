Simple Sponge Cake

Ingrediants

  • 3 Egges
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Caster Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
Self raising Flour
A pre mixed combination of flour and leaving agents ( usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 C.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the egges and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flourC.
  5. Pour mixture in to the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated ovcen ,or until the top of the cake spring back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
